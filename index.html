Franchise Models We Offer

Choose a model that fits your ambition. Whether you want to run a full-fledged retail store or join as a delivery partner — we have options tailored to your goals.

Retail Outlet

The flagship model of our brand, perfect for high-footfall urban and semi-urban locations. Fully equipped with billing systems, product shelving, and inventory software. This model ensures a strong brand presence and offers the highest potential returns over time due to a comprehensive product range and customer loyalty.

  • Area Required: 400–800 sq.ft
  • Investment: ₹12–20 Lakhs
  • Returns: 18–24 Months

Kiosk Model

Ideal for malls, railway stations, and market places — this low-investment model is easy to manage and fast to scale. A quick-setup option that still delivers strong brand recall and consistent profits with optimized inventory.

  • Area Required: 80–100 sq.ft
  • Investment: ₹5–8 Lakhs
  • Returns: 12–16 Months

Delivery Partner

Start your journey as a delivery logistics partner without the need for a physical store. Leverage our brand and delivery management system to grow in your local area. Best suited for college students, part-timers, or existing courier partners looking to expand into grocery delivery

  • Area Required: Not Required
  • Investment: ₹1–2 Lakhs
  • Returns: 6–9 Months

Master Franchise

Take the leadership role in an entire zone or city. You’ll be responsible for setting up sub-franchises, training, and regional marketing. This model suits business veterans and investors looking to control large-scale operations with higher ROI potential and brand leverage.

  • Area Required: Based on Region
  • Investment: ₹25 Lakhs+
  • Returns: 24–30 Months
