How to Become a Franchise Partner

Follow these simple steps to start your journey as a franchise partner and build a successful business with our support.

1

Step 1: Submit Your Application

Fill out a simple form with your basic details and area of interest. This helps us understand your profile.

2

Step 2: Initial Discussion

Our franchise manager will connect with you for an introductory call to understand your expectations and resolve queries.

3

Step 3: Visit & Evaluation

You’re invited to visit our existing franchise or head office to experience the operations firsthand.

4

Step 4: Agreement & Onboarding

After mutual approval, legal documentation is done and onboarding begins with initial training and setup support.

5

Step 5: Grand Launch

With our team’s support, your store is launched and starts serving customers as part of the growing brand family.
