Franchise Models We Offer

Choose from flexible franchise formats that suit your goals and investment plans. We offer end-to-end support, so you can focus on growth.

Kiosk Model

Ideal for malls, transit hubs, and high-footfall areas. Quick setup with minimal space requirements and a faster return on investment. Perfect for first-time business owners.

Space: 80–100 sq. ft | Investment: ₹5–8 Lakhs

Retail Outlet

A complete grocery store setup with modern interiors, comprehensive inventory, and full branding support. Suitable for entrepreneurs aiming at long-term stability and higher profitability.

Space: 400–800 sq. ft | Investment: ₹12–20 Lakhs

Delivery Partner

Join our last-mile delivery team and earn a flexible income. A great opportunity for individuals looking for low-investment entry into the grocery business with no space requirement.

No Space Needed | Investment: ₹1–2 Lakhs

Master Franchise

Take control of a large territory, manage multiple sub-franchisees, and lead regional operations. Ideal for seasoned business professionals aiming for a high-growth, leadership-driven opportunity.

Regional Rights | Investment: ₹25 Lakhs+
