How to Become a Franchise Partner

Take the first step towards building a successful business with our easy-to-follow process and full support every step of the way.

1

Step 1: Submit Your Application

Complete a straightforward form with your contact details and location preferences to help us understand your interests and suitability.

2

Step 2: Initial Discussion

Our franchise manager will connect with you for an introductory call to understand your expectations and resolve queries.

3

Step 3: Visit & Evaluation

We’ll invite you to visit one of our existing franchises or our head office so you can experience the operations and see how everything works in real time.

4

Step 4: Agreement & Onboarding

Once we mutually agree, we proceed with the legal documentation, followed by comprehensive onboarding that includes initial training and setup support to get your franchise ready for success.

5

Step 5: Grand Launch

With our team’s support, your store is launched and starts serving customers as part of the growing brand family.
