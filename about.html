About Us – MegaMart99

We empower aspiring entrepreneurs with a proven franchise model, extensive support, and a strong brand reputation in the retail industry.

Trusted by
1,00,000+ Customers

MegaMart99 is a next-generation supermarket brand, dedicated to offering a wide range of high-quality products at smart and affordable prices.

What makes us different? Every product at MegaMart99 is uniquely priced with an MRP ending in 99, delivering unbeatable value on every purchase.

At MegaMart99, you’ll find everything under one roof — home care, stationery, cosmetics, décor, gifts and toys, personal care, baby care, footwear, apparel, accessories, electronics, tools, pooja needs, car accessories, and daily grocery essentials. We make shopping easy, affordable, and enjoyable for every customer.

We also specialize in bulk gifting solutions for businesses and institutions, offering special discounts, custom printing, and personalized packaging for corporate and festive orders.

Beyond retail, MegaMart99 empowers franchise partners with full store setup support, including POS systems, billing software, printers, weighing scales, barcode scanners, baskets, trolleys, racks, and display units. We ensure complete branding and promotional support to help every store succeed.

With a strong focus on quality, variety, affordability, and customer satisfaction, MegaMart99 isn’t just a supermarket — it's a complete retail ecosystem, designed for today’s smart shoppers and future-ready entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer everything under one roof—from daily grocery needs to lifestyle products—while maintaining the highest standards in service, quality, and variety. We are also committed to supporting corporate and industrial clients through bulk supply solutions with customized printing, packaging, and exclusive discounts. Additionally, we provide full infrastructure and promotional support to retail partners, ensuring a strong foundation for success.
